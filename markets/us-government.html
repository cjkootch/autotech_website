Frequently Asked Questions

What federal compliance standards must government technology solutions meet?
⌄

Federal technology must meet rigorous standards including FISMA (Federal Information Security Management Act), NIST cybersecurity frameworks, 508 accessibility compliance, ADA requirements, data protection regulations, and specific agency policies. We design solutions that meet or exceed all applicable federal, state, and local compliance requirements, from initial architecture through implementation and ongoing support.

How do you manage the unique procurement and budget cycles in government?
⌄

We understand government procurement processes including FedRAMP, GSA schedules, and state/local contracting requirements. Our experience spans capital project planning, multi-year appropriations, federal grant funding, and budget justification documentation. We work within government fiscal calendars and can structure solutions to align with your existing procurement frameworks and approved vendor relationships.

What's your approach to legacy system modernization without disrupting operations?
⌄

Our modernization approach uses phased migration, parallel running, and rollback-ready implementations to minimize operational disruption. We conduct thorough data audits, create detailed conversion plans, maintain legacy system access during transitions, and provide comprehensive testing and validation before cutover. Throughout implementation, your staff maintains service continuity while gaining modern capabilities.

How do you address interoperability across multiple government agencies?
⌄

We design systems around industry standards and secure APIs that enable data sharing across agency boundaries while protecting sensitive information. Our solutions support common data exchanges, consolidated reporting, and cross-agency coordination without requiring agencies to abandon their existing platforms. We help establish governance frameworks for secure, authorized data sharing.

What ongoing support and training do you provide to government staff?
⌄

We provide comprehensive training tailored to different user roles (administrators, operators, supervisors, executives), create detailed system documentation and standard operating procedures, establish help desk protocols, and offer ongoing technical support. We train your "train the trainer" team to ensure sustainable knowledge transfer and reduce future support dependencies.
